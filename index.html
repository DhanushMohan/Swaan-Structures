About Us

Transforming visions into Reality

Swaan Structures is a dynamic Engineering Construction Company based in Coimbatore Established in 2012 . With a relentless commitment to excellence, we specialize in the design and construction of both Reinforced Cement Concrete Buildings (R.C.C) and Steel Buildings. Our diverse portfolio includes R.C.C construction, PEB (Pre-Engineered Buildings) Steel Structures, Project Management Consultancy, and Valuation services for R.C.C and Steel Buildings. Swaan Structures is committed to transforming visions into reality, all while upholding our commitment to affordability and waste prevention
